Frequently Asked Questions

What are the shipping options?

We offer standard and express shipping. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 1-3 business days. All orders are processed within 24 hours.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. To initiate a return, please visit our contact page and send us a message.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on the carrier's website to track your package.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and times vary by destination.
